General Experience & Reflections

The most challenging part of this project was juggling all the different roles and making sure everything stayed organized. I was able to overcome it by breaking things down and taking it one step at a time. The part I enjoyed most was seeing everything come together and actually publishing the sites. If I could redo the project, I’d start earlier and communicate even more clearly. After doing this, I definitely feel more confident working on real-world projects because I’ve had hands-on experience with planning, teamwork, GitHub, and getting a site live.
